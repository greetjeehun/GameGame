AND -- A Normal Defence

Defend your home from the enemies!!!






How to play

Use up arrow, down arrow key to move up and down. Go to the lane where enemy tries to invade your home and kill him with your sword.

Explanation

You are the last warrior in your village that has survived from the attack from the evil monsters. You must fight for your life and your families lives for as long as possible! You cannot give up hope! However the monsters forces get stronger as time goes by. Don't allow the monsters to get to any of the homes in the village or its Game Over!
